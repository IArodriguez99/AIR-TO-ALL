Our first product is the Air One PAPR (powered air-purifying respirator) to protect frontline workers from COVID19.

SAFETY FEATURES

Fail safes include an alarm for low battery life. The user can still breathe through the filter if the fan shuts down. Filtered air is completely sealed off from ambient air.

FILTER

The Air One PAPR features a HEPA filter that is 99.97% efficient against 0.3-micreon particles, protecting against a wide range of harmful airborne particles.

BATTERY

The PAPR uses a rechargeable Li+ battery that lasts 8-10 hours. The battery can be recharged in <2.5 hours.

HOOD

The loose fitting reusable hood provides 150% more protection vs. an N95 mask, including eye protection and low breathing resistance. It accommodates a range of head sizes and can be comfortably worn over eyewear and facial hair.
